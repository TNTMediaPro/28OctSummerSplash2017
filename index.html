About Summer Splash

The Summer Splash designed and created to celebrate the urban culture of the people of Soweto and surrounding areas. An annual program that will culminate a spectacle of social cohesion, promotion of music, arts industries as well as support of micro business sectors. The Summer Splash is a one of its kind experience that is designed to expose the richness and talent of Soweto.

The Summer Splash gives an opportunity to draw different people from neighboring towns and provinces; the youth at large, artists, organizations and businesses, media and tourists around Gauteng in unity on an annual basis to celebrate Soweto’s richness, diversity, culture and the ever-growing entertainment. An outdoor picnic featuring a lineup of SA’s finest artists, deejay’s, emcee's and personalities. The Summer Splash will attract fun loving influencers with a strong social media presence, the best hip hop, house music, and afro pop music entertainment show elevating our guests to new turn up heights.

This 14 hour non-stop outdoor Summer Splash will host and entertain all our guests in style, we are looking at a 1-day program that will embrace and reward the audience through a multi theme of events for the young and old.

LOCATION

 Soweto,  Mofolo Park

The location of the event will be held at, what we know as Mofolo Park. This is a well-known area and is situated in the heart of the Soweto community, which is situated in Mofolo Township.

Despite it being one of the largest urban residential areas in Gauteng, the formal economy of Soweto is small and the most affected is the youth; which makes them despondent and also increases the crime rate.

Soweto is a well-known urban area with a victorious history. It has bred and nurtured legends from different spheres of life, from political leaders, leading businessmen and women, to phenomenal artists young and old. There is great activity in and around Soweto and this event creates an even better exposure to what Soweto is and has to offer.

The Summer Splash aims to provide a cultural and entertainment related to businesses with a professional event for Soweto’s Artists to showcase their talents and services. In turn, it delivers a phenomenal, fun community festival where everyone can enjoy and pride themselves.

The Summer Splash aims to provide a one of a kind profitable event for everyone, but mostly the residents of Soweto.


Aims and Objectives

Our event aims at promoting Soweto as a global brand. A lot of work is put into the final product, which aims to leave a mark and an unforgettable experience for all stakeholders. • Profile, promote and grow Soweto.

 • Build reputation and the Soweto brand. 

• To promote domestic tourism with Soweto as a key strategic destination.

 • To create a picnic that attracts large numbers of people from Soweto as the festival is about attracting South Africa and the world at large. 

• Honor and celebrate those who put Soweto on the Map.

 • Develop appropriate and ethical networks and relationships with stakeholders that enhance the image of Soweto as a whole. 

• The expertise, products and materials used shall be of high quality to ensure excellence.  

• Acceptable standards shall be met. Facilitation and production of this event shall be annual and progressive.

 • Adherence to the terms and conditions of the contract as and when in agreement with the Municipality, Legislature and Stakeholders. This will strengthen Soweto as a destination market for entertainment while educating people about the culture and heritage of Soweto and also inspiring the youth to be globally driven and exposing SA talent is our core mission.
